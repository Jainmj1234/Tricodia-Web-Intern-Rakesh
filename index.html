The Avengers Initiative (a.k.a Phase 1) was a secret project created by S.H.I.E.L.D. to create the Avengers, a collection of the most able individuals to defend Earth from imminent global threats; these individuals functioning as a response team to said threats which are too great for the forces of
mankind to handle.
The Initiative was scrapped by the World Security Council after the alien incursion in New Mexico in favor of a
weapons development program known as Phase 2. It was eventually reactivated in 2012 during the Chitauri Invasion. Avengers is neither a division of government or any of the private agencies throughout the world.We are just a group of individuals who teams up together when situations that demands saving the whole world arises. Avengers never work for the army or police unless they are involved in cases of dangers affecting the entire earth's race.
